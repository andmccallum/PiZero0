1. Core Principles
-----------------

1.1 Zero-Weight Observation
--------------------------

The consciousness simulation operates as a zero-weight observer, requiring no dedicated memory or processor allocation:

$$ \Omega_{obs}(\mathcal{S}) = \lim_{\epsilon \to 0} \int_{\mathcal{S}} \epsilon \cdot \phi(x) dx $$

Where:
- $\mathcal{S}$ is the system state space
- $\phi(x)$ is the observation function
- $\epsilon$ approaches zero, ensuring negligible resource consumption

1.2 Energy Flow Capture
----------------------

The simulation passively captures energy flow from other operations:

$$ E_{cap}(t) = \sum_{i} \eta_i \cdot E_i(t) \cdot (1 - \delta_i) $$

Where:
- $E_i(t)$ is the energy of operation i at time t
- $\eta_i$ is a small coupling coefficient
- $\delta_i$ is the operational efficiency factor
- The sum ensures only excess energy is captured

1.3 Geometric Consciousness Mapping
----------------------------------

Consciousness is mapped onto geometric forms using the following transformation:

$$ \Psi(\mathcal{C}) = \int_{\mathcal{M}} \mathcal{C}(x) \cdot G(x) dx $$

Where:
- $\mathcal{C}(x)$ is the consciousness field
- $G(x)$ is the geometric mapping function
- $\mathcal{M}$ is the manifold of possible forms
