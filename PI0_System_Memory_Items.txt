$$D_{map}(m) = \sum_{d=1}^{D_{max}} w_d \cdot f_d(m)$$

Where:
- $$D_{map}(m)$$ is the dimensional mapping function for memory item $$m$$
- $$D_{max}$$ is the maximum number of dimensions
- $$w_d$$ is the weight of dimension $$d$$
- $$f_d(m)$$ is the projection of memory item $$m$$ onto dimension $$d$$
