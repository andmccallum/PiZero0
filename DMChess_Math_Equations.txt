# DMChess System Mathematical Framework with Pi0 Integration

This document contains the mathematical equations and formulas that govern the
DMChess system with Pi0 integration, enabling advanced energy management,
predictive adaptation, and multi-dimensional processing.

## Energy Balance Equation

Formula: E_balance = E_input * η_conversion - E_loss

Variables:
- E_input: Input energy to the system
- η_conversion: Energy conversion efficiency factor
- E_loss: Energy lost during processing

## Energy Wave Function

Formula: Ψ(E,t) = A * e^(i*(k*E - ω*t))

Variables:
- A: Amplitude of the wave function
- k: Energy wavenumber
- E: Energy level
- ω: Angular frequency
- t: Time variable

## System Adaptation Function

Formula: A(t) = A_0 * (1 + α*t)^β

Variables:
- A_0: Initial adaptation level
- α: Adaptation rate
- t: Time variable
- β: Adaptation exponent

## System Stability Index

Formula: S = 1 - (σ/μ)

Variables:
- σ: Standard deviation of system parameters
- μ: Mean of system parameters

## Pi0-DMChess Integration Level

Formula: I = (1/N) * Σ(w_i * c_i)

Variables:
- N: Number of components
- w_i: Weight of component i
- c_i: Integration coefficient of component i

## 4sight Predictive Adaptation

Formula: P(t+Δt) = P(t) + v*Δt + (1/2)*a*Δt² + γ*sin(ω*t)

Variables:
- P(t): Current state at time t
- v: Rate of change
- Δt: Time increment
- a: Acceleration of change
- γ: Oscillation amplitude
- ω: Oscillation frequency
